Mizuki & Shoya

How simple, yet mystical
The love that you give
Swirling, punctual
Impossible to relive

The world, letting out her roar
Downing its weight on us, we see
But always and forevermore
Our two hearts could make it easy

We are warriors, fighting only with a shield
Conquering the grotesque and the breeze
For life is a battlefield
But our tenderness is the peace

When the rains pour down quieter
And ocean waters subside
My heart feels like summer
Waking next to you at sunrise
